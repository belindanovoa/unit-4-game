Fruit Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four fruits below. By clicking on a fruit you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lost the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each fruit is hidden from you and its up to you to calculate the correct score.
